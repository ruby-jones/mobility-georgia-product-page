How does Rent-to-Own work?

You pay a fixed monthly amount over 6 months. After your 6th payment, you own the bed outright. There is a 15% convenience premium included in the total — this is clearly shown before you check out.

Why is the mattress purchased separately?

Mattresses are a hygiene-sensitive item and cannot be returned or rented. They must be purchased in full at the time of delivery. This is the same policy regardless of whether you purchase or rent-to-own the bed.

Can I return the bed?

Yes — you may decline the item before our delivery team leaves at no restocking fee. Within 7 days after delivery, eligible items may be returned with a 15% restocking fee, or converted into a Rent-to-Own agreement.

What areas do you deliver to?

We serve Metro Atlanta and a 100-mile radius including Athens, LaGrange, Villa Rica, Gainesville, and Peachtree City. Delivery & setup is free within 15 miles and $450 for longer distances.

You may decline the item before the delivery team leaves — no restocking fees, only the delivery fee applies.

After delivery: steel hospital bed frames qualify for a 100% refund (excluding delivery fees) within 7 days if in perfect condition.

Other items may be returned within 7 days with a 15% restocking fee, or converted to a Rent-to-Own contract.

Mattresses, once unwrapped or used, cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

Contact us by phone or chat to request a Return Authorization. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after inspection.
